About This Application

RadioIndia – Live FM and Music is a radio directory application developed by Corneon AI. The application allows users to discover and listen to publicly available internet radio stations.

Radio Stream Sources

This application does not host, store, or rebroadcast any audio streams on its own servers.

All radio streams available in the application are publicly accessible streaming links provided by the respective radio broadcasters or authorized stream providers.

The app simply acts as a directory that helps users access these publicly available streams.
