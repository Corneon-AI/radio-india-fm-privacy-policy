Radio India : Live FM and Music · Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Radio India : Live FM and Music is a radio streaming app published by Corneon AI. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected and how it is used.

1) Information We Collect

Radio India : Live FM and Music does not require users to create an account and we do not ask for or collect personal information such as your name, phone number, address, contacts, or payment information.

The app may automatically collect limited technical information such as:

  • App activity & performance data (usage events, crash logs, diagnostics)
  • Device information (device model, OS version)
  • Network information (connection type)
  • Advertising identifier (only if ads are enabled)

2) How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain streaming functionality
  • To improve performance and fix crashes
  • To enhance user experience
  • To display ads (only if enabled)

3) Radio Streams & Content

Radio India : Live FM and Music provides access to publicly available radio stream links from third-party providers. We do not host or control radio streams unless explicitly stated. Availability and content are managed by the respective radio stations.

4) Permissions

  • Internet: required to stream radio content
  • Media / playback controls: used to support background playback and media controls (if enabled)

5) Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, or advertising (if enabled). These services may collect limited technical information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6) Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Limited technical information may be shared only to operate and improve the app (for example, analytics or crash reporting if enabled).

7) Data Retention

Technical information is retained only as long as necessary to maintain the service, analyze performance, or comply with legal obligations.

8) Security

No method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable safeguards.

9) Your Choices

  • You may stop using or uninstall the app at any time
  • You can limit advertising identifiers in device settings (if ads are enabled)

10) Children’s Privacy

Radio India : Live FM and Music is not directed toward children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time and revise the “Last updated” date accordingly.
