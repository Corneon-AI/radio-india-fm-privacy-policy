Radio India : Live FM and Music · Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Radio India : Live FM and Music is published by Corneon AI. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it may be used, and how third-party radio content is handled in the app.

1) Information We Collect

Radio India : Live FM and Music does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information such as your name, phone number, contacts, location, or payment information.

The app may automatically collect limited technical information such as:

  • App performance data (crash logs and diagnostics)
  • Device information (device model and operating system version)
  • Network information (connection type required for streaming)
  • Advertising identifiers (only if advertising services are enabled)

2) How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain radio streaming functionality
  • To monitor and improve app performance
  • To diagnose crashes and technical issues
  • To improve overall user experience
  • To display advertisements if advertising services are enabled

3) Third-Party Radio Streams

Radio India : Live FM and Music is a directory and player for publicly available internet radio streams.

The app does not host, upload, rebroadcast, or store any radio content. All radio streams are provided by their respective broadcasters and remain hosted on the broadcasters’ servers.

All trademarks, logos, station names, and audio content belong to their respective owners.

If you are a radio station owner and believe your stream has been included without permission, you may contact us and we will promptly review and remove the station if requested.

4) Permissions

  • Internet Access – Required to stream radio content from broadcasters
  • Media Playback Controls – Enables background playback and system media controls

5) Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as analytics, crash reporting, or advertising services to help improve the app experience. These services may collect limited technical data according to their own privacy policies.

6) Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Limited technical information may be processed by trusted service providers only for the purpose of operating, maintaining, and improving the app.

7) Data Retention

Technical information collected for analytics or diagnostics is retained only as long as necessary to maintain app functionality, improve performance, and comply with legal obligations where required.

8) Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect technical information. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

9) User Choices

  • You may stop using the app at any time
  • You may uninstall the app to stop all app-related data collection
  • You may restrict advertising identifiers in your device settings if ads are enabled

10) Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11) Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When updates occur, the “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly.
